Rajasthani Bajre Ki Roti

Traditional millet flatbread from the desert state of Rajasthan

Prep Time

20 minutes

Cook Time

25 minutes

Servings

6 rotis

Calories

180 per roti

Ingredients

  • 2 cups bajra (pearl millet) flour
  • 1/2 cup whole wheat flour
  • Hot water for kneading
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • Extra ghee for serving

Instructions

  1. Mix bajra flour, wheat flour, and salt in a bowl
  2. Add hot water gradually and knead into a soft dough
  3. Add 1 tbsp ghee and knead again
  4. Divide into 6 portions and roll into rotis
  5. Cook on hot tawa with ghee until golden spots appear
  6. Serve hot with ghee
