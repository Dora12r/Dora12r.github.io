Traditional Panch Phoron Dal

A classic Bengali dish made with five unique spices and slow-cooked lentils

Prep Time

15 minutes

Cook Time

30 minutes

Servings

4 people

Calories

245 per serving

Ingredients

  • 1 cup masoor dal
  • 1 tsp panch phoron
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • 2 dry red chilies
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 cups water

Instructions

  1. Wash and soak dal for 30 minutes
  2. Heat ghee in a pan and add panch phoron
  3. Add onions and garlic, sauté until golden
  4. Add dal and water, cook until tender
