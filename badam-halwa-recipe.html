Mughlai Badam Halwa

A rich and aromatic royal dessert from the Mughal kitchen

Prep Time

30 minutes

Cook Time

40 minutes

Servings

6 people

Calories

385 per serving

Ingredients

  • 2 cups almonds, blanched and ground
  • 1 cup pure ghee
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • A few saffron strands
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • Sliced almonds for garnishing

Instructions

  1. Soak almonds overnight, peel and grind to fine paste
  2. Heat ghee in a heavy bottom pan
  3. Add almond paste and cook on low heat
  4. Add sugar and milk mixed with saffron
  5. Cook until mixture leaves sides of pan
  6. Add cardamom powder and mix well
  7. Garnish with sliced almonds and serve warm
